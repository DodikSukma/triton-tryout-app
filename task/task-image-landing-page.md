# 🎨 Image Tasks for Triton Denpasar Landing Page

Dokumen ini berisi panduan prompt AI untuk menghasilkan gambar ilustrasi modern yang digunakan pada halaman landing Bimbel Triton Denpasar. Anda dapat menggunakan DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, atau generator gambar lainnya dengan prompt di bawah ini. Semua gambar dispesifikasikan agar memiliki background putih polos (solid white background).

---

### 1. Hero Illustration (Hero Section - Right Column)
**Prompt:**
> "A cheerful flat vector illustration of diverse Indonesian high school and middle school students studying together around a table. One student is raising their hand confidently, another is writing notes, one is using a laptop. A friendly teacher stands nearby pointing at a whiteboard with math equations. Surrounding the scene are floating icons: graduation caps, stars, trophies, books, and lightbulbs. Color palette: sky blue, white, navy blue, with warm orange accents. Clean modern edtech style, no text in image, solid white background. 3:2 aspect ratio."
